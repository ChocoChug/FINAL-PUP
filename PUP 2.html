QUEZON CITY

Started as petitions to have a higher educational institution for the youth at the National Government Center area from the community organizations and church groups and negotiations with local officials and other government agencies, the PUP Quezon City Campus is a pantheon of concrete success towards the struggle in people’s fight against ignorance. As enshrined on the Deed of Assignment on 24 March 1997, that is, “to establish a branch or an extension of PUP at the National Government Center with a mandate to bring quality education and subsidized tuition fees to the urban poor communities of Quezon City, with priority given to those from the NGCHP.”

MISSION

Ensuring inclusive and equitable quality education and promoting lifelong learning opportunities through a re-engineered polytechnic university by committing to:

  • Provide democratized access to educational opportunities for the holistic development of individuals with a global perspective.
  • Offer industry-oriented curricula that produce highly skilled professionals managerial and technical capabilities and a strong sense of public service for nation building.
  • Embed a culture of research and innovation.
  • Continuously develop faculty and employees with the highest level of professionalism.
  • Engage public and private institutions and other stakeholders for the attainment of social development goal.
  • Establish a strong presence and impact in the international academic community.

VISION

PUP: The National Polytechnic University

GOALS

Transparency and Efficiency:

  • Responsibly manage school-related activities that may lead to the holistic development of all stakeholders in instruction, research, and extension.


System Performance:

  • Leadership, collaboration, and strategic partnership in institutional, national, and international activities.


Organizational Excellence:

  • Taking accountability in delivering quality service through excellent performance of faculty members and administrative staff.


Safety and Health:

  • Promote health through worthwhile school activities involving faculty members, staff, and students.

ACADEMIC PROGRAMS


Graduate Studies

Undergraduate Degrees

Master in Information Technology (MIT)

This program focuses on advanced topics in Information Technology, including software development, network management, and database systems.

Duration: 2 years

Master in Educational Management (MEM)

This program is designed for educators and administrators seeking advanced knowledge in educational leadership and management.

Duration: 2 years

Master in Public Administration (MPA)

This program covers public administration principles, policy analysis, and governance for individuals aiming for leadership roles in the public sector.

Duration: 2 years

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT)

This program focuses on the design and implementation of software applications, network systems, and information databases.

Duration: 4 years

Bachelor of Business Technology and Livelihood Education major in Home Economics (BBTLEDHE)

This program combines business and home economics education to prepare students for teaching and related careers.

Duration: 4 years

Bachelor of Business Technology and Livelihood Education major in Information Communication and Technology (BTLEDICT)

This program integrates business and ICT education to equip students with skills for teaching and the ICT industry.

Duration: 4 years

Bachelor of Science in Business Administration major in Human Resource Management (BSBA-HRM)

This program focuses on the principles and practices of human resource management within the business context.

Duration: 4 years

Bachelor of Science in Business Administration major in Marketing Management (BSBA-MM)

This program emphasizes marketing strategies, consumer behavior, and market research within the business environment.

Duration: 4 years

Bachelor of Entrepreneurship (BSENTREP)

This program provides students with the knowledge and skills needed to start and manage their own businesses.

Duration: 4 years

Bachelor of Public Administration major in Public Financial Management (BPAPFM)

This program focuses on public financial management, budgeting, and fiscal policies in the public sector.

Duration: 4 years

Diploma in Office Management Technology Medical Office Management (DOMTMOM)

This diploma program specializes in medical office management, covering administrative tasks in healthcare settings.

Duration: 2 years
